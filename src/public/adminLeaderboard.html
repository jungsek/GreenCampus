Points Calculation

Schools earn points based on their monthly and yearly performance in two categories:

  • Carbon Footprint (CF)
  • Energy Usage (EU)

Total Score

A school's total score is the sum of points from both Carbon Footprint and Energy Usage categories. The maximum possible score is 200 points (100 from each category).

Ranking Changes

The arrows next to each school's score indicate changes in rank compared to the previous period:

  • ↑ : Improved rank
  • ↓ : Decreased rank
  • - : No change in rank
