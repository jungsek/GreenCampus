Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

General

What is GreenCampus about?
GreenCampus is an online portal which tracks energy consumption, monitors carbon emissions, and visualizes goals for reducing environmental impact. It helps schools analyze resource usage, promote environmental awareness, and foster sustainable habits among students.

Who is GreenCampus designed for?
GreenCampus is designed for schools, students, and staff who want to take measurable steps toward creating an eco-friendly campus and build awareness about sustainability.

Login

How do I sign up for GreenCampus?
Enter your first name, last name, email and a password, before selecting your account type (student or admin).

Logging data

What equipment is needed to use the platform?
Users only need access to a computer or smart device with internet connectivity. For automated tracking, smart meters or compatible systems may be required.

How do I input data?
The data can be integrated from school energy systems, smart meters, or manually entered, depending on the available infrastructure.

Campaigns

How do I create a campaign?
Enter a campaign name, description, and select the number of campaign points.

Reports

Can I generate more than one report?
Yes, users can generate multiple reports.
