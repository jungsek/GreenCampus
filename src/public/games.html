Greendle

Test your knowledge of environmental words! Try to guess the 5-letter word in as few attempts as possible.

How to Play:

  • Type a 5-letter word and press ENTER
  • The color of the tiles will change to show how close your guess was:
A
 Letter is correct and in the right position
E
 Letter is in the word but in the wrong position
G
 Letter is not in the word

Scoring:

Reward points are awarded based on how quickly you guess the word:

  • 1st try: 10 reward points
  • 2nd try: 7 reward points
  • 3rd try: 5 reward points
  • 4th try: 3 reward points
  • 5th try: 1 reward point
  • Not solved: 0 reward points

Pop Quiz

Test your environmental knowledge with our eco-friendly quiz!

How to Play:

  • Choose an environmental topic that interests you.
  • Answer 5 multiple-choice questions about your chosen topic.
  • Get immediate feedback and explanations for each answer.
  • Learn interesting facts about environmental conservation.

Scoring & Rewards:

  • Points are awarded based on total correct answers:
    • 1 correct answer: 1 point
    • 2 correct answers: 3 points
    • 3 correct answers: 5 points
    • 4 correct answers: 7 points
    • 5 correct answers: 10 points
  • Points are only awarded for your first quiz of the day
  • You can play additional topics for practice
